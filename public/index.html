Calculation of UEFA Country Ranking

The UEFA Country Ranking (also known as the UEFA coefficient) allows comparison of the strength of national associations. A country's total coefficient = sum of its annual coefficients over the last 5 seasons. Each season's coefficient = total club points ÷ number of clubs entered.
